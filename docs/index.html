Habit Garden is built to improve your life. From daily tasks to getting rid of unwanted routines and building new habits, you are now in control and ready to make a change

Attractive and intuitive, this personal tracker is aimed for all people. Are you a busy worker who wants to spend his free time more wisely? Or are you a student who regularly forgets doing his assignments? Worry not, we got you covered

Building healthy habits is the key of success, but it requires dedication. However, we've made this process fun and pleasant by pursuing the idea of planting flowers. And it's really easy. Plant your flower, take care of it and see the results. Your progress will be seen in the number of your planted flowers. The more you have, the better.
